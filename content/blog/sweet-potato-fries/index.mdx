## Recipe

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

Line half sheet pan with parchment paper. Cut potato into into fry shapes.
They can be whatever shape you want, but the sizes should be similar for even cooking.
Put potato in large bowl and add olive oil and toss to coat. Less oil is better here.
Add seasonings while tossing and transfer to sheet pan.

Bake for 15 minutes and wang jangle. Repeat until have desired color and texture.
