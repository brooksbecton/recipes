## Recipe

Saute onions until translucent then cook garlic.
Add rice and toast for 1 - 2 minutes. The rice should be slightly translucent around the edges.

Add 1 cup at a time and stir in until the broth has been absorbed.
Keep adding broth until the rice has the desired texture.

Cut heat and stir in cheese and butter.

### Vegetables

- Mushrooms - Cook with onions
- Frozen Peas - Add before adding cheese and butter

### Protein

- Rotisserie Chicken
- Leftover Roast
- Left Over Sausage
